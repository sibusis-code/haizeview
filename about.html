About Us

Your Trusted Safari Companion in Mpumalanga

Hazyview Exclusive Tours is a professional tours and travel transportation company based in Hazyview, Mpumalanga. We specialize in airport transfers, safari adventures, guided excursions, village experiences, and transportation services throughout the Kruger National Park region.

Our experienced drivers and guides ensure every journey is safe, memorable, and authentically African — showcasing the natural beauty and cultural richness of South Africa.

Community Impact

Tourism That Gives Back

Our village visits connect travellers with the remarkable local communities surrounding the Kruger National Park. Visitors experience genuine Tsonga culture while supporting disadvantaged schools and families through voluntary donations and community engagement.

This initiative is built on social impact, not profit. Every visit is a small act of bridge-building between the world's visitors and the people whose land and heritage make this region so extraordinary.
